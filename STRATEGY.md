# The Flow Hub — Strategy House

**Model:** Erhvervsudlejning

## Vision (rooftop)

**Vi muliggør at industrien kan producere bedre**

Empowering the industry to produce better — offering the most inspiring workplace and flowtech community in Northern Europe.

## Strategic purpose

To build an inspiring workplace, a strategic asset for our core business and a sustainable investment.

## Core mission: differentiation

The Flow Hub positions MODU Valves as much more than a regular valve dealer. No competitor has anything like this. A purpose-built, architecturally significant HQ in wood and recycled materials signals ambition, permanence, and values that a rented warehouse never will.

This differentiation doesn't happen by itself. It must be deliberately planned and executed on — through content, PR, customer visits, partner events, and every touchpoint where The Flow Hub can shift how people perceive MODU.

## Why The Flow Hub as a content channel

The Flow Hub is a separate brand from MODU Valves. That unlocks content that doesn't belong on MODU's channels.

**Local ambassadors.** The more local people who hear about MODU, our family, and what we're building, the more ambassadors we get. Their likes and shares reach their own networks — which may include connections at interesting customers we can sell to. Indirect reach, zero sales pressure.

**Free shoutouts to partners.** We can give genuine exposure to Nordicals, RAVN Arkitektur, and all underleverandører til The Flow Hub. This content doesn't make sense on MODU Valves — our valve customers don't care who laid the bricks. But on The Flow Hub, it's natural. The partners appreciate it and engage back. Low effort, high love.

**A named building creates a connection.** When you give a building a name, people form a relationship with it. "The Flow Hub" is not an accident — it lets us talk about our core business (flow technology, ball valves) and sustainability (because of the building's ESG profile) through a brand that feels personal, not corporate. The positive associations with the building rub off directly on MODU Valves without us having to sell anything.

**Higher resale value.** If we ever sell the building, we believe it will be worth more as "The Flow Hub" — a named property with its own LinkedIn profile, custom-built software for booking the rooftop and meeting rooms, and fully automated maintenance outreach to VVS, tømrer, etc. A buyer inherits a turnkey operation, not just a building. And if we don't sell, we run it ourselves with near-zero overhead.

**More attractive to tenants.** We believed the remaining 1,400 m² would be easier to rent out as "The Flow Hub" rather than "MODU HQ". Tenants want a neutral, appealing address — not to sit inside someone else's headquarters. This was the primary reason for giving the building its own brand, and why it got its own color palette instead of reusing MODU's. The founder's original idea was to make it a "Competency Hub for the flow industry", which shaped the name and identity. That concept was later abandoned, but the brand stuck — and turned out to serve the rental and content strategy even better.
